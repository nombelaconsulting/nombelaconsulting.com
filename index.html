About Us

Nombela Consulting is a South African-based advisory firm that supports purpose-driven organisations in achieving financial clarity and social impact. Founded by Ndumiso Ngwabi, CA(SA), the firm offers tailored services in accounting, ESG advisory, and financial reporting. We bring international experience and a values-driven approach to every client engagement.

Founder

Ndumiso Ngwabi – CA(SA), Founder & Principal Consultant

Ndumiso brings experience from top firms including Deloitte, BDO LLP in London, PwC LLP (via Makosi), and is currently a Director at the One K Foundation. His work has spanned asset management, audit, and the non-profit sector, with a strong emphasis on financial reporting, stakeholder engagement, and social impact.
