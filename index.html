About Us

Nombela Consulting is a forward-thinking South African advisory firm specialising in aligning financial clarity with sustainable impact. We support mission-driven organisations, from non-profits to impact-focused enterprises, by delivering solutions that are financially sound and ethically grounded.

Founded by Ndumiso Ngwabi, a Chartered Accountant (SA) with global experience across Africa, the U.S., and Europe, we bring insights from top audit firms like Deloitte, PwC, and BDO. Our expertise spans ESG strategy, corporate reporting, sustainability assurance, and impact measurement.

Whether you're integrating ESG frameworks, enhancing financial transparency, or designing an impact strategy, Nombela Consulting provides trusted guidance rooted in global standards and local relevance.

Our Team

Ndumiso is a Chartered Accountant (SA) and Senior Sustainability Advisor at The ESG Institute. With over seven years of international audit and advisory experience, he has worked on engagements involving financial integrity, ESG strategy, and non-financial reporting.
