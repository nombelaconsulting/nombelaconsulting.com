About Us

Nombela Consulting is a South African-based advisory firm that helps purpose-driven organisations align financial clarity with sustainable impact. Founded by Ndumiso Ngwabi, CA(SA), our firm blends deep expertise in accounting, ESG strategy, and social impact to deliver practical, values-led solutions. With global audit experience from firms like Deloitte Africa, PwC LLP in New York, and BDO LLP in London, we understand the highest standards of financial governance.

Our Team

Ndumiso Ngwabi – Founder & Principal Consultant
Chartered Accountant (SA) | Senior Sustainability Advisor – The ESG Institute

Ndumiso brings over 7 years of international audit and advisory experience, having worked with firms and organisations across South Africa, the United Kingdom, and the United States. His work spans ESG reporting, financial management, impact-driven advisory, and corporate governance. He currently also serves as a Director at The One K Foundation.
